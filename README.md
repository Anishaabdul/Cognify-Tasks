# Restaurant Data Analysis Project
# Cognify Data Analytics Tasks
1. Project Overview
This project analyzes a Restaurant Dataset to uncover insights about:
Top cuisines
City-wise restaurant distribution
Price range trends
Online delivery availability
Ratings and votes analysis
Cuisine combinations
Geographic patterns
Reviews behavior
Table booking trends
The objective is to perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and generate business insights that can help restaurant aggregators and food platforms improve strategy.

---
# Project Objectives
Identify most popular cuisines
Analyze restaurant distribution by city
Study price range patterns
Examine impact of online delivery
Understand restaurant ratings and votes
Explore cuisine combinations
Perform geographic analysis
Compare price range with delivery & table booking

# Analysis Performed
# Top Cuisine Analysis
Extracted and cleaned cuisine data
Counted frequency of cuisines
Identified most popular cuisines
Insight: Certain cuisines dominate across multiple cities.

# City-wise Restaurant Analysis
Grouped restaurants by city
Identified cities with highest restaurant density
Insight: Metro cities have significantly higher restaurant presence.

# Price Range Distribution
Analyzed distribution of restaurants across price categories
Visualized count of restaurants by price range
Insight: Majority restaurants fall under mid-range pricing.

# Online Delivery Analysis
Compared restaurants offering online delivery vs not
Analyzed ratings for both categories
Insight: Restaurants offering delivery tend to have higher engagement.

# Restaurant Ratings Analysis
Examined rating distribution
Identified highly rated restaurants
Studied rating trends across cities
Insight: Most restaurants cluster between 3.0â€“4.0 ratings.

# Cuisine Combination Analysis
Analyzed multi-cuisine restaurants
Identified popular cuisine pairings
Insight: Certain cuisine combinations appear frequently (e.g., fast food + beverages).

# Geographic Analysis
Used latitude & longitude for spatial distribution
Visualized restaurant concentration
Insight: Restaurants are densely located in central urban zones.

# Reviews & Votes Analysis
Studied relationship between votes and ratings
Identified highly voted restaurants
Insight: Higher votes generally correlate with higher ratings.

# Price Range vs Online Delivery & Table Booking
Compared delivery availability across price ranges
Compared table booking trends across price segments
Insight:
High-price restaurants more likely to offer table booking
Mid-range restaurants more likely to offer online delivery
